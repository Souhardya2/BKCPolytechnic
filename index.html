Located in Bhubaneswar, the temple city of India, Bkc Polytechnic is a Premier Polytechnic established in the year 1997 offering Diploma in Engineering and Technology. Located amidst serene surrounding the Institute offers 3 year Diploma Engineering in three streams. Bkc Polytechnic has very good infrastructure with spacious well ventilated well lit and well equipped Class Rooms, Workshops, Laboratories and Conference Hall along with well stocked library.
